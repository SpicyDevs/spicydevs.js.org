SpicyDevs Server - Rules

SpicyDevs - Spice it Up

These rules must be followed when communicating in the Spicydevs Server.

  • Must follow Discord Terms-Of-Service.

    You must follow the Discord Terms and Guidelines, while communicating in our Server.

  • Spamming in Chat/Ticket or Spam Pinging other Members/Staffs.

    If we find you Spamming reactions, messages, emotes, walls of text, zalgo text, newlines, and pings in chat is prohibited. Spamming new posts in form channels, or spamming tickets. If you are caught doing the following, SpicyDevs has the right to punish you with mute/kick/ban.

  • No Malicious content or Scamming

    Sending viruses and also sending IP grabing links are strictly prohibited. Any link that is sent intentionally to record IP addresses will result in punishment. This also includes hosting your own website and asking “who’s xxx.xxx.xxx?” while sending someone’s IP. Don’t use Discord to scam or send malware / viruses

  • Leaking Private Information.

    We do not allow any personal information to be sent into the server. This will include: IPs, Addresses, Phone numbers, Legal names, as well any other identifiable information. Any private information regarding an user without their consent may lead to ban.

  • No NSFW Content / Gore / Being Horny

    Do not share any NSFW content, sites, or any other messages related to NSFW. Don’t talk about sex (or any other sexual activity), genitals, kinks, fanfiction, or any other content meant to inflict arousal will lead to moderation. No NSFL or gore content Videos or content that is NSFL (not safe for life, or things that nobody wants to see), or discussion about these topics, is strictly prohibited.

  • No Advertising.

    Sending an invite link, telling people to join your server, intentionally pointing to your status or about me that contains an advertisement is strictly prohibited. We do not allow advertising on this server. Advertising also includes other websites, or any other links.

  • Your Discord Profile must adhere to our rules

    Any NSFW, racist, hate speech, sexist, or malicious content on your profile will result in you being punished. This includes your: name, bio, and your pronouns. Impersonation of any staff members or No Text To Speech for malicious reasons or as a joke will result in you being punished.

  • Be Respectful to All Members

    Don’t troll, be toxic, cause arguments, or be rude to members. And also don’t be racist, sexist, send death threats, or engage in hate speech

  • Use Appropriate Channels.

    Post content in relevant areas. Off-topic discussions belong in designated channels.

  • No Discrimination.

    SpicyDevs is inclusive; avoid discrimination based on any factors.

  • Respect Moderator's/Staff's Decisions. / Listen to Moderators/Staffs.

    Follow instructions from moderators without resistance. Follow guidance from moderators for a positive community experience.

  • Don’t Find Loopholes in the Rules

    No rules list is completely cohesive. If you feel like you’ve been treated unfairly by a moderator, please report it to an admin. If you feel like you’ve been treated unfairly by and admin, DM @aayu5h, or @fake.dark.dev. If you intentionally are finding loopholes or misreporting abuse to staff, you will be punished accordingly.
